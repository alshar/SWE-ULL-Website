{# TODO: Clean this up and add it to the page. This is directly copied from the old website #} Our goal for Spring 2019 is to provide housing for 20 students at the conference. This housing will be in a hotel within walking distance to the conference center in which WE19 will be hosted, with free continental breakfast and Wifi. It will be a shared commodity available from the evening of Wednesday, November 6th to the morning of Sunday, November 10th. For this housing, SWE UL is fundraising this spring, through the summer, and into the fall with events such as SWE Snacks on the Street and Give-Back Nights. The money raised is directly proportional to the hours spent and preparations made from volunteers. A herculean effort from over a dozen individuals is required to pull together the funding to attend WE19, and that's why volunteers with a minimum of 30 points earn housing to the conference from SWE UL. How to get volunteer points? Sign up in the Events page of this website for shifts and show up. With remaining funds, SWE UL will also reimburse registration fees after the conference to those volunteers with over 30 points. We will maintain this system on a merit basis, so the more you volunteer, the more financial aid you receive. Any cheating or attempts at cheating will disqualify you from any financial assistance (see more in “The Ethics of the Volunteer Point System”).
