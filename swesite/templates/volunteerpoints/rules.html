Volunteer Point Rules

We want everyone to

Don't lie about your participation in an event.
This includes:

  • Sending someone to take your place
  • Not staying for the full time you committed to.
  • Showing up, taking a picture, and leaving if taking a picture is a requirement for event credit

We shouldn't have to say this, but please act professionally at all events.
Some examples of acting unprofessionally include:

  • Fighting with fellow volunteers or attendees.
  • Attending an event intoxicated.
  • Not showing up to an event without letting a SWE Officer know
  • Doing anything you wouldn't do at work, school, or any other professional setting
