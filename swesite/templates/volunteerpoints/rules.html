Volunteer Point Rules

We want everyone to

Don't lie about your participation in an event.

This includes:

Sending someone to take your place.
  • If you sign up, you have to complete the allotted time you committed to.
  • “forgetting about the date/time/place” is not a valid excuse and is considered unethical.
  • Please remember that many of our events double as tours or programs that have limited seating, and that you would take the opportunity away from someone else by not attending.
Showing up and leaving early.
  • To receive credit for some of our events, we ask that you post pictures of your work to our Facebook page.
  • Do not sign up, show up, snap a picture, and then leave.
